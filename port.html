About Umair Ahmad

A detail-driven Mechanical BIM Engineer with real international project experience, a strong simulation background, and the technical depth GCC employers are looking for.

Professional Profile

I am a Mechanical BIM Engineer and MEP Revit Modeler & Detailer with hands-on professional experience at BIMTEQ, a Pakistan-based firm serving American clients in Houston, USA. I have modeled, coordinated, and detailed full MEP systems — HVAC ducting, chilled water, ventilation, and plumbing — for multi-floor international projects at LOD 400–450.

HVAC Ducting & Piping

Specialized in HVAC Ducting Modeling & Detailing and Piping Systems Design using Autodesk Revit MEP. I develop accurate 3D duct layouts, piping networks (chilled water, water supply, drainage), and coordinate all systems to avoid clashes before construction — saving time and cost on site.

BIM Coordination & Clash Detection

Using Autodesk Navisworks, I perform full multidisciplinary BIM coordination — identifying and resolving clashes between mechanical, electrical, plumbing, architectural, and structural models. I prepare detailed clash detection reports and coordinate with all discipline teams to ensure zero-conflict construction documents.

Simulation & Engineering Analysis

Beyond BIM, I have strong skills in SolidWorks (Advanced — 3D modeling, assemblies, motion study, FEA), ANSYS (Advanced — structural, thermal, CFD/CFX), ABAQUS (Intermediate — explicit dynamics), and COMSOL Multiphysics (Intermediate — multiphase CFD). These skills make me a versatile mechanical engineer capable of both design and analysis.
